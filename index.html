Welcome to New Leaf

We are committed to providing a variety of Fairtrade goods and products to our customers.
We are based in Aberdeen, but hope to expand our services to other retail stores and also to provide an online store in the near future.
If you look through our site you will find listings of our currently available products, which include Fairtrade Groceries, Clothing and Gifts. If you would like to more about us or find out where we are located you can check our About page. Or if you need to Contact us for any reason, there is a Contact page with our phone, e-mail and social networking options.
